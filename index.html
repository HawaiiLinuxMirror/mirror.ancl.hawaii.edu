Advanced Network Computing Laboratory Mirror

The goal of the Advanced Network Computing Laboratory mirror is to be one of the largest mirrors in the world with very fast download pipes for people in Hawaii. This mirror (as of 2013) has the largest total hard drive space of any mirror in the world.
